Welcome to the NZ Fiscal Policy Simulator

You are the Minister of Finance for New Zealand. Each quarter, set direct tax, indirect tax, public services, and infrastructure investment. The simulator will show the fiscal preview first. Once you press OK, shocks and policy flow through aggregate demand and the AD/AS model.

Direct tax
Lowering direct tax increases disposable income and can improve investment incentives, shifting AD right.
Indirect tax / GST
Lowering indirect tax makes consumption cheaper and can reduce short-run price pressure.
Public services
Teachers, police, nurses, and other services count as G and raise AD quickly.
Infrastructure investment
Infrastructure counts as G now and can ease supply pressure over time.
